MassKara Festival

Bacolod City – October

The MassKara Festival is held every October in Bacolod City, known as the "City of Smiles." The word “MassKara” is a combination of “mass” (crowd) and “kara” (face), and the festival was born during a time of crisis to uplift the spirits of the people.

The event is famous for its vibrant street dancing where participants wear smiling, colorful masks, elaborate costumes, and perform synchronized dances to upbeat music.

It’s a joyful celebration that showcases the Bacolodnons’ resilience, optimism, and creativity, and has become one of the most iconic and anticipated festivals in the country.
