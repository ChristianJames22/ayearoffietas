Peñafrancia Festival

Naga City – September

The Peñafrancia Festival is a major religious celebration held every September in Naga City, Camarines Sur, in honor of Our Lady of Peñafrancia, the patroness of the Bicol Region.

The highlight of the festival is the fluvial procession, where the image of the Virgin Mary is paraded down the Naga River on a pagoda surrounded by hundreds of candle-lit boats, accompanied by fervent prayers and hymns.

The weeklong festivities also include a civic-military parade, novenas, religious rituals, and cultural shows. It draws millions of devotees and pilgrims, making it one of the largest Marian celebrations in Asia.
