Dinagyang Festival

Iloilo City – 4th Sunday of January

Dinagyang Festival is Iloilo City’s spectacular religious and cultural festival held in honor of the Santo Niño and to celebrate the arrival of Malay settlers and the subsequent selling of Panay Island to them by the Atis.

The event is famous for its fierce, energetic street dancing performed by warriors in full-body paint and elaborate tribal costumes, along with booming drum beats and synchronized chants.

Dinagyang is a multi-award-winning festival recognized both locally and internationally for its creativity, organization, and passionate community involvement.
