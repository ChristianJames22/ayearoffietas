Sinulog Festival

Cebu City – 3rd Sunday of January

The Sinulog Festival is one of the grandest and most colorful festivals in the Philippines, held every January in Cebu City in honor of the Santo Niño (Child Jesus).

Participants clad in vibrant costumes dance to the rhythm of drums, trumpets, and native gongs in a lively procession that mimics the traditional Sinulog dance — two steps forward, one step back — symbolizing the Filipinos’ journey of faith.

A mix of religious devotion and cultural celebration, the Sinulog Festival draws millions of visitors every year, transforming the streets of Cebu into a dazzling display of unity, tradition, and joy.
