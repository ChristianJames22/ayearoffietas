Black Nazarene Procession

Quiapo, Manila – January 9

The Feast of the Black Nazarene is one of the most solemn and widely attended religious events in the Philippines, held annually on January 9 in Quiapo, Manila.

Devotees clad in maroon shirts walk barefoot as a sign of humility and penitence, joining a massive procession called the "Traslación" that transports the life-size image of the Black Nazarene through the streets of Manila.

This event draws millions of participants and is known for its deep expression of Filipino faith, sacrifice, and fervent devotion to Jesus Christ.
