Kaamulan Festival

Malaybalay City, Bukidnon – March

The Kaamulan Festival is an ethnic-cultural celebration held in Malaybalay City, Bukidnon. It honors the customs and traditions of the seven indigenous tribes of Bukidnon: Bukidnon, Higaonon, Talaandig, Manobo, Matigsalug, Tigwahanon, and Umayamnon.
