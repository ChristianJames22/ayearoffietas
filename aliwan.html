Aliwan Fiesta is dubbed the "Mother of All Fiestas" in the Philippines. Held annually in April, it brings together the best cultural festivals from across the country in one grand competition in Metro Manila.

The event features street dancing, floats adorned with local products, and beauty pageants. Participants represent their provinces, showcasing the rich diversity and traditions of their region through music, costume, and choreography.

Aliwan Fiesta aims to promote cultural tourism and national pride, uniting Filipinos in a spectacular celebration of heritage and creativity.
